Do I need experience in finance?+
No. Many people start with no financial background. Training and mentorship are part of the path, and a willingness to learn matters more than a resume.
Can I start part-time?+
Many people begin part-time around an existing job or family commitments and grow from there. The intro call is the place to talk through what works for you.
